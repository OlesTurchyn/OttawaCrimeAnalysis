Visualizing Ottawa's Shooting and Firearm Discharges

An interactive heatmap visualizing data from 2016 to 2022

Heatmap Visualization

This heatmap shows the areas with a high concentration of shooting and firearm discharges in the city of Ottawa.

Temporal Analysis

Incidents by Year

This chart shows the distribution of shooting incidents across different years. We can see a significant decrease in 2020 - likely due to the COVID pandemic. However, in 2021, there was a significant increase.

Incidents by Month

The chart shows a higher number of incidents in the summer months, especially June through August.

Incidents by Day of the Week

The data shows that shooting incidents tend to occur more frequently on weekends, particularly on Fridays and Saturdays. This could indicate a correlation with social activities or gatherings.

Incidents by Hour of the Day

Most incidents are reported in the evening hours, peaking at around 10pm. This pattern might reflect higher levels of social activity and gatherings during these hours.
